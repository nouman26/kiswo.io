About Us

Khidmat Insaniat Social Welfare Organization works for needy and deserving people. We carry out our social and welfare projects on self funding and members contribution. Helping Needy and Poor People is Main Mission and Goal of this Organization, from Providing food to ration , providing small businesses opportunities to legal advises this organization works for needy people on priority basis

Our Mission

  • To Provide free rations on a monthly basis to the homes of poor and widowed women so that they can avoid starvation
  • To Provide free education to orphans and deserving childrens in villages
  • Free ambulance service for the poor and needy people
  • Free food distribution for the poor and travelers in different districts of sindh on a daily basis
  • Distribute free wheelchairs to poor and disabled people
  • Give free sewing skills to deserving women so that they can learn sewing and earn a living in their own home with dignity and become self-sufficient
  • Giving the unemployed a small business so that they can earn a living and fulfill their home expenses
  • Free legal advice and counsel Needy and Deserving People Who Cannot afford a lawyer in court can hire the lawyer without any fee. Khidmat Insaniyat Welfare Organization Provide them free lawyers and legal advice to pursue their cases and legal matters
